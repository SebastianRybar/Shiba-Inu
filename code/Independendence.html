Independendence

Embracing the Independence of Shiba Inus


Personality Traits

Self-Reliance: Shiba Inus are often described as cat-like due to their independent and sometimes aloof demeanor. They are perfectly content to spend time alone and can entertain themselves.

Confidence: This breed is confident and assertive. Shiba Inus are not typically clingy and may not seek constant attention from their owners.

Interactive Toys and Activities

Puzzle Toys:Provide puzzle toys that stimulate their mind and keep them engaged. These can help satisfy their curiosity and need for mental stimulation.

Solo Activities: Encourage activities that Shiba Inus can enjoy alone, such as chew toys, which cater to their independent play style.
