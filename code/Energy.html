Understanding and Managing the High Energy of Shiba Inus


Energy Levels

High Energy: Shiba Inus are high-energy dogs that require regular physical and mental stimulation to prevent boredom and behavioral issues.

Alertness:They are naturally alert and curious, always ready to explore their surroundings and react to any changes.

Exercise Needs

Daily Exercise:Shiba Inus need at least an hour of exercise each day. This can include walks, playtime, and off-leash running in a secure area.

Variety: Incorporate a variety of activities such as hiking, running, and fetch to keep your Shiba Inu engaged and challenged.
