Shiba-Inu

Basic informations: Shiba Inu, originating from Japan, is known for its fox-like appearance with erect ears, a curled tail, and a plush double coat. They are spirited and independent, often aloof with strangers but loyal to their families. Highly energetic, Shibas need regular exercise and enjoy activities like running and hiking. Naturally clean, they groom themselves like cats but shed heavily seasonally, requiring regular brushing. A unique trait is their distinctive "Shiba scream," a loud sound they make when unhappy, scared, or excited.
